**Effective Date:** August 1, 2026

## Our Promise

We want you to love your DENBA Recovery Balance Wear. If for any reason you're not satisfied, we offer returns and exchanges under the conditions below.

## 1. Return Window

Items may be returned or exchanged within **14 days of delivery**. The 14-day period starts on the date marked as "delivered" by the courier (DHL Express tracking).

## 2. Eligibility

Items are eligible for return if:
- ✅ Unworn and unwashed
- ✅ All original tags attached
- ✅ Original packaging intact
- ✅ Returned within 14 days of delivery

Items are **not eligible** if:
- ❌ Worn, washed, or altered
- ❌ Tags removed
- ❌ Damaged due to customer handling
- ❌ Sale or final-sale items (clearly marked at checkout)
- ❌ Returned after 14 days

## 3. How to Initiate a Return

1. Email us at **info@denba-style.jp** with:
   - Order number
   - Item(s) you wish to return
   - Reason for return (optional but helpful)
2. We will respond within 2 business days with return instructions and a return address.
3. Ship the item via tracked mail to the address provided.
4. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund or exchange within 5 business days.

## 4. Return Shipping Costs

- **Customer-initiated returns** (size change, change of mind): Customer pays return shipping.
- **Defective items, wrong item shipped, our error**: We cover return shipping (we will provide a prepaid DHL Express label).

## 5. Refund Processing

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Processing time: 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
- For DDP (delivered duty paid) orders, original duties and taxes are non-refundable per customs regulations.

## 6. Exchanges

Size exchanges are subject to availability. If the requested size is out of stock, we will issue a refund instead.

## 7. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:
1. Email us within 7 days of delivery with photos
2. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you

All international orders ship **DDP (delivered duty paid)**. This means:
- ✅ All duties and import taxes are calculated at checkout

- ✅ No surprise charges on delivery
- ✅ No customs forms or hold-ups at your local post office

Once dispatched, you'll receive an email with your DHL Express tracking number. Track in real time at [dhl.com](https://www.dhl.com/).

- Military addresses (APO/FPO)

If your package is marked as delivered but you have not received it, contact us within 7 days. We will open an investigation with DHL.
