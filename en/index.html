FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Japan Class I Medical Device" mean?
The Medical Line is registered as a Class I medical device under Japan's Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare (Reg. No. 13B3X10395000005). Within the registered scope, the Japanese product can carry the claims "supports blood circulation" and "supports post-activity recovery." Outside Japan, the product is sold as wellness apparel and has not been evaluated by the U.S. FDA.
What is the difference between Thermalux™ and Balancetech™?
Thermalux™ is a graphene-based fabric technology that uses your body heat to emit far infrared rays, delivering a deep sense of warmth. Balancetech™ is a fabric technology infused with DENBA's proprietary mineral blend, designed to support awareness of your body's central axis while wearing it. Our products use both technologies together.
Do the functional properties last after washing?
Yes. Both Thermalux™ and Balancetech™ are woven into the fabric itself, so the properties remain as long as the fibers are not damaged. Please avoid chlorine bleach and high-temperature tumble drying, as these can damage the fabric.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes — unopened and unused items can be exchanged for a different size within 14 days of delivery. Please contact our customer support (info@denba-style.jp). Return shipping is the customer's responsibility.
Can I wear it outside of sleep?
Absolutely. It's designed for comfortable wear during relaxation, around the house, and during light daytime activity. The relaxed silhouette also works well as loungewear.
Are there any cautions for use?
Please consult a physician before use if you are pregnant, undergoing medical treatment, wearing implanted electromedical devices such as a pacemaker, or have skin conditions. Discontinue use immediately if you experience any discomfort.
How does worldwide shipping work?
We ship worldwide via DHL Express (220+ countries). Orders are dispatched within 3–5 business days and typically arrive within 2–5 business days after dispatch. All international orders are shipped DDP (delivered duty paid), so duties and taxes are calculated and included at checkout — no surprise charges on delivery.
