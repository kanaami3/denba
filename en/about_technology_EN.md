# Our Story

## Three decades of materials research, woven into every garment.

DENBA JAPAN Inc. is a Tokyo-based technology company building on **30+ years of research into materials, energy, and human wellness.** Our parent technology group has applied its proprietary expertise across industries — from food preservation to medical equipment to consumer wellness.

**DENBA Recovery Balance Wear** is the result of that long arc of research, translated into something you can wear, every day.

We don't make ordinary apparel. Every thread in our garments carries a piece of our engineering — graphene that turns your body heat into deep warmth, minerals that quietly support your awareness of your own posture. The technologies are invisible, but the experience is unmistakable.

---

# Two Proprietary Technologies

## Thermalux™ — Warmth from your own body.

A graphene-based fabric technology. Graphene fibers, woven into the textile, emit far infrared rays using your body heat as the source.

- **No batteries. No external energy.**
- **Activates the moment you wear it.**
- **Maintains its properties for the lifetime of the garment.**

In Japan, garments using Thermalux™ in the Medical Line are registered as Class I medical devices, with approved claims supporting circulation and post-activity recovery within the registered scope.

*Outside Japan, all products are sold as wellness apparel. Not evaluated by the U.S. FDA. Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.*

## Balancetech™ — Body awareness, woven in.

A fabric technology infused with DENBA's proprietary mineral blend. The minerals create a subtle physical presence on the skin — not heavy, not stiff, but persistent enough to bring your attention quietly to your own posture and center.

- **The minerals don't move your body. You do.**
- **But while you wear it, you become more aware of how you move.**
- **In clinical terms, this awareness is called proprioception.**

The longer you wear it, the more it becomes part of your daily rhythm.

# Our Commitments

## Quality
Every garment is manufactured under DENBA quality oversight at certified facilities. Our Medical Line is held to Japan's pharmaceutical-grade documentation standards.

## Transparency
We tell you what the technology does — and what it doesn't. Our claims are bounded by what the science supports and what regulators have approved in each market.

## Sustainability
We are working toward fully recyclable packaging and lower-impact production. This is an ongoing journey, and we will publish progress updates as our supply chain evolves.
