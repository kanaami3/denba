## 📦 1. PRODUCTS & TECHNOLOGY

**Q: What makes DENBA Recovery Balance Wear different from other recovery apparel?**
A: We're powered by two proprietary fabric technologies developed over 30+ years of research: Thermalux™ (graphene-based far-infrared fabric) and Balancetech™ (mineral-infused postural-support fabric). Our Medical Line is registered as a Class I medical device in Japan.

**Q: What is Thermalux™?**
A: Thermalux™ is a graphene-based fabric technology. Graphene fibers, woven into the fabric, emit far infrared rays using your body heat as the source — creating a deep, enveloping sense of warmth.

**Q: What is Balancetech™?**
A: Balancetech™ is a fabric technology infused with DENBA's proprietary mineral blend. The fabric maintains a gentle physical presence on the skin, supporting your awareness of your body's central axis (proprioception) while you wear it.

**Q: Do all products use both technologies?**
A: The Medical Line (Recovery Balance Pajama) uses both Thermalux™ and Balancetech™. The Standard Line (T-shirt, Long Sleeve, Hooded Parka, Parka Pants) uses Balancetech™ only.

**Q: Is this a medical device?**
A: The Recovery Balance Pajama is registered as a Class I medical device in Japan (Reg. No. 13B3X10395000005). Outside Japan, all our products — including the Pajama — are sold as wellness apparel and have not been evaluated by the U.S. FDA or equivalent authorities in other countries. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

**Q: Are the materials safe for sensitive skin?**
A: All our fabrics are tested to OEKO-TEX standards for skin safety. The graphene and mineral materials are woven into the fiber itself — there is no chemical coating that can rub off. That said, if you have severe skin sensitivities, please patch-test before extended wear.

## 🧺 6. CARE & MAINTENANCE

**Q: How do I wash my DENBA wear?**
A: Machine wash in a laundry net at 40°C / 104°F or below. Hang in shade — do not tumble dry. The fabric properties do not wash out.

**Q: Can I use bleach?**
A: No — please avoid chlorine bleach, which can damage the fabric.

**Q: Can I iron it?**
A: Yes — up to 160°C / 320°F, with a press cloth recommended.

**Q: How long do the functional properties last?**
A: Both Thermalux™ and Balancetech™ are woven into the fabric itself, so the properties last for the lifetime of the garment as long as the fibers are not damaged.

**Q: How should I store it long-term?**
A: Keep in a well-ventilated place away from direct sunlight. Avoid plastic bags for long-term storage — natural fibers need to breathe.

## 🩺 7. SAFETY & PRECAUTIONS

**Q: Are there any safety warnings I should know about?**
A: Please consult a physician before use if you are:
- Pregnant or nursing
- Undergoing medical treatment
- Wearing implanted electromedical devices (pacemaker, defibrillator, insulin pump, etc.)
- Have any skin conditions or allergies
Discontinue use immediately if you experience any discomfort.

**Q: Can children wear it?**
A: Our smallest size (XS) is designed for adults of smaller stature. We do not currently offer children's sizes.

**Q: Is it safe during pregnancy?**
A: Please consult your physician before use during pregnancy.

**Q: Can I wear it during exercise?**
A: Yes — the Balancetech™ technology is designed for daily wear including light activity (walking, yoga, golf). For intense workouts, the relaxed fit may feel loose; we recommend a more fitted base layer for high-intensity training.
